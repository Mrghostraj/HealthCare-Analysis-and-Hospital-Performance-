# Healthcare Analytics & Hospital Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Healthcare Analytics solution developed using **Power BI** and **Python**. The objective was to analyze patient demographics, insurance coverage patterns, healthcare claims, revenue recovery, and hospital financial performance to support data-driven decision-making.

The project consists of two interactive dashboards:

1. **Patient Demographics & Insurance Analytics Dashboard**
2. **Hospital Performance & Revenue Analytics Dashboard**

The analysis was performed on healthcare datasets containing over **130,000+ records**, including patient information, insurance details, claims, billing transactions, and payment records.

# Dashboard 1: Patient Demographics & Insurance Analytics

## Objective

To analyze patient demographics, insurance coverage, and geographic distribution of patients.

## Key Metrics

* Total Patients
* Male Patients
* Female Patients
* Average Age

## Analysis Performed

### Patient Demographics

* Gender Distribution
* Ethnicity Distribution
* Marital Status Analysis
* Average Age Analysis

### Insurance Analytics

* Patients by Insurance Provider
* Insurance Coverage Distribution

### Geographic Analysis

* Patients by State
* Patients by City

### Interactive Filtering

* Insurance Provider
* State
* Ethnicity

## Key Insights

* Female patients represent the majority of the patient population.
* Hispanic patients form the largest ethnic group.
* California contributes the highest number of registered patients.
* Insurance coverage is relatively balanced across major providers.

# Dashboard 2: Hospital Performance & Revenue Analytics

## Objective

To evaluate hospital financial performance through claims, billing, payment recovery, and insurance analytics.

## Key Metrics

* Total Revenue
* Recovered Revenue
* Recovery Rate
* Total Claims
* Denied Claims
* Approval Rate

## Analysis Performed

### Revenue Analysis

* Total Billed Revenue
* Total Recovered Revenue
* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Revenue by Insurance Provider

### Claims Analysis

* Claim Status Distribution
* Paid vs Denied Claims
* Approval Rate Analysis
* Denial Analysis

### Financial Performance

* Recovery Rate Evaluation
* Payment Method Analysis
* Revenue Recovery by Insurance Provider

### Interactive Filtering

* Claim Status
* Insurance Provider
* Payment Method

## Key Insights

* Over 113M in billed revenue was analyzed.
* Revenue recovery performance was evaluated across insurance providers.
* Claims approval and denial trends were monitored to identify operational inefficiencies.
* Payment methods and insurer performance were compared to support financial planning.
